mat3

Header file: include/mat3.h

mat3 is a column-major matrix with 3 columns and 3 rows.

\(mat3 = \begin{bmatrix} x.x & y.x & z.x \\ x.y & y.y & z.y \\ x.z & y.z & z.z \end{bmatrix}\)
